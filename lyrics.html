Namavalis & Songs




On this page, we have the lyrics of some of Muruga's popular songs and namavalis. Muruga has the unique privilege of making the listeners also sing along with him and chant the 'Bhagavan Nama'. Many such listeners get into trace which is many times witnessed in Marudamalai Padi Thiruvizha and in Ramakrishna Vidhyalaya, Coimbatore. He has written numerous songs in Tamil praise of Godess Kanyakumari, Lord Muruga and Lord Krishna.

Devi Amma also has written numerous songs in Tamil and Malayalam in praise of Lord Krishna, Lord Ayyappa, Goddess Paramaikavu Bhagavathy, and Lord Shiva
