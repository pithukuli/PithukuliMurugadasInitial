As Thiru Valluvar says


"Seviku unavu illadha pozhudhu siridhu
vayitrukkum iyapadum"


"When there is no food for the ears some food
is given to the stomach"

Muruga always sings accompanied by his harmonium. By divine grace of the Goddess, notes that flow from his tongue and notes from his harmonium are always in sync. Namavalis flow like a gushing river with ease and elan in Muruga's bhajans. Muruga encourages all the listeners to join him in all the namavalis. Here are some songs that Muruga has sung on various occasions. Lyrics of these songs could be found on the Lyrics page. It is just a small tip of an enormous iceberg!
